🔍 Data Authenticity Principles

This dashboard follows a strict source-priority hierarchy. All data is tagged with a confidence level (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW) and linked to its raw snapshot. Source tiers: (1) Official REST/JSON APIs → (2) Official CSV/XLSX → (3) Official HTML → (4) Official PDFs → (5) Trusted secondary sources.
